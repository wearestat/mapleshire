
Tower Hamlets Document links

The Borough Profile 2024 brings together the key facts and figures about Tower Hamlets into one report. It outlines the rapid growth and constant change in our population.
It offers details on our diverse communities. This includes ethnicity, birth country, and religion. It also covers employment, economy, health, education,
 transport, poverty, housing and crime.
https://www.towerhamlets.gov.uk/Documents/Borough_statistics/Tower-Hamlets-Borough-Profile-2024.pdf

Pupil Attitude Survey (PAS)
The Tower Hamlets Pupil Attitude Survey focuses on pupils’ views and experiences on health and well-being, staying safe, the use of technology, 
local community and plans for the future. As the only comprehensive platform where 
young people from the borough can share their views and experiences of their lives, the survey provides invaluable data.
https://www.towerhamlets.gov.uk/lgnl/community_and_living/borough_statistics/Education.aspx

The higher education journey of young Tower Hamlets residents
This report was produced by London Councils with Continuum, University of East London and the London Borough of Newham and is part of a London-wide programme of research around destinations of young Londoners.  The report provides analysis of the higher education journey of young Tower Hamlets residents aged 18-24 years, from their pre-HE institutions, through their higher education study, and on to their graduate employment destinations.

The HE journey of young Tower Hamlets residents
https://www.towerhamlets.gov.uk/Documents/Borough_statistics/The_HE_journey_of_young_Tower_Hamlets_residents_June_2016.pdf
